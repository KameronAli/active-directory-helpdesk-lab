User and Group Structure

Organizational Units:
- IT
- HR
- Sales
- Accounting
- Disabled Users
- Security Groups

Example Users:
- IT user accounts placed under IT OU
- HR user accounts placed under HR OU
- Sales user accounts placed under Sales OU
- Accounting user accounts placed under Accounting OU

Security Groups:
- HR-Share-Access
- Sales-Share-Access
- Accounting-Share-Access
- IT-Admins or IT-Support

Purpose:
Groups were used to manage access to shared folders instead of assigning permissions directly to individual users.
